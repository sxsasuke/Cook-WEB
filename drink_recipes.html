Drink Recipes
Discover the world of flavors
The culture of making drinks is the whole universe. Coffee, tea, cocoa are classic drinks for breakfast. Compotes, fruit drinks, juices — for lunch. It's not a sin to drink a cocktail in the evening. And there are also seasonal drinks: winter and warming (glegg, grog, punch or mulled wine) and refreshing for hot summer (lemonade, krushon or sangria). It is impossible to embrace the immensity, but we tried to collect as many drink recipes on the site as possible, building convenient navigation by type and ingredients. You will find those drinks that can be prepared for the winter, preserving the taste of summer berries and fruits, and a variety of ways to prepare coffee and tea blends, and momentary fantasies that do not require professional bartender skills like "Bloody Mary" or mojito.
