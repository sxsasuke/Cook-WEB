Main course Recipes
Discover the world of flavors
The main course is the culmination of any lunch or dinner. Usually it is meat, poultry or fish cooked whole or in portions in the oven or in a frying pan. For vegetarians, cereals and legumes come to the fore, hearty and substantial, able to compensate for the lack of carbohydrates and protein. The most popular recipes in this category are cooked chicken in all possible ways. Tobacco chicken, teriyaki chicken, pilaf with chicken, chicken with beer — on the website you will find both classic chicken dishes and their author's interpretations, as well as a side dish and sauce suitable for the dish. Meat in the form of steaks, stews, goulash and French, beloved by many, is no less popular as a main dish. Fish and seafood require special attention, so it's worth adding recipes for fish with marinade or shrimp in curry to your piggy bank. Finally, minced meat dishes are not forgotten: cutlets, lasagna, casseroles, cabbage rolls. Proven recipes will help you create a varied menu for every day and on a special occasion — classic recipes for main dishes for an anniversary, New Year or a children's holiday.
