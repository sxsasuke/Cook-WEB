Salad Recipes
Discover the world of flavors
In the form and variety to which we are accustomed, salads exist only in Russia and nowhere else. What should I eat with the first glass? Vinaigrette! What is the New Year without? Without Olivier. What to treat the guests on the Eighth of March? "Mimosa", yellow and fluffy, like a spring flower itself. Recipes of classic Soviet salads have a worthy place on the site. But if you want to change the picture to something less familiar and not so heavy, pay attention to the English Waldorf salad, Greek salad with feta and Maghreb tabouleh salad with couscous. An interesting salad can even be made from potatoes, and each time it will sound in a new way: with bacon, onion and mustard in the German manner, with green beans and an apple, as in the Liege salad from Belgium, or in a combination beloved by the British — with tuna and cucumber. A separate conversation about really festive, elegant salads: with crabs, shrimps, mussels, red caviar and red fish, chicken and duck — the site will find not only their recipes, but also beautiful serving options. And, most importantly, any salad is not a rigid set of ingredients, but rather a planned plot that is easy to develop as you like. The same salad leaves can be mixed not with fresh, but with burnt tomatoes, and the salad with squid can be supplemented with smoked cheese — you will also find these recipes on the website and can use them as a starting point of your own imagination.
