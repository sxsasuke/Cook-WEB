FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register on the website?

You can register by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner and following the instructions.

How can I find a specific recipe?

Use the search bar at the top of the page to type in the name of the recipe you're looking for.

Can I leave comments and reviews on recipes?

Yes, you can leave comments and reviews on each recipe page if you are logged in.

How can I find restaurants that serve a specific dish?

In the "Restaurants" section, you can find information about restaurants that serve various dishes.

The website is not working correctly. What should I do?

If the website is not working correctly, please contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes, you can save your favorite recipes by clicking the "Save" button on the recipe page.

How can I share a recipe with my friends?

You can share recipes using the share buttons available on each recipe page.

How can I customize the portion size of a recipe?

You can customize the portion size by using the portion size adjuster available on the recipe page.
